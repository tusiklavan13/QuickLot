About QuickLot

QuickLot is a fast futures position sizing tool. Enter your risk (USD) and stop (ticks) → get exact contracts, with both before-fees and after-fees views. You can pick a broker/prop schedule and we auto-fill round-turn fees.

Highlights:

  • Search symbols quickly (datalist) + ⭐ favorites
  • Broker/Prop fee presets (edit anytime)
  • Local save of your last inputs (no account needed)
  • Clean mobile-first UI

Roadmap: TP targets by entry & side, slippage in ticks, points↔ticks conversion, PWA install, JSON-based auto updates for fees & volatility.
